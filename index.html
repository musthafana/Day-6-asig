Admission Guidelines

Age Requirement:

Applicants must meet the age criteria for the grade they are applying to. For example, children applying for kindergarten must be at least 5 years old by September 1st of the admission year. This ensures that all students are developmentally ready for the curriculum.


Residency Status:

Applicants must reside within the school's designated catchment area or provide proof of their intention to move to the area before the start of the school year. This helps maintain a community-focused student body and ensures that resources are allocated to local families.


Previous Academic Records:

Applicants must submit academic records or report cards from their previous school(s) for the past two years. These records provide insight into the students academic performance and help place them in the appropriate grade level and programs.


Immunization Records:

Applicants must provide up-to-date immunization records or a valid exemption certificate. This is essential for maintaining the health and safety of all students and staff, ensuring compliance with public health regulations.
